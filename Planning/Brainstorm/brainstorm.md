### idea 1

**SG60pop: Memory Match**

## What it is:

A vibrant, Singapore-themed memory game celebrating SG60 using pop-art icons like the Dragon Playground and Merlion.
It focuses on clean UI and smooth CSS animations.

## How it works:

**The Grid:**
Players face a 4x3 grid of face-down cards.

**The Logic:**
Flip two cards at a time. If they match, they stay up; if not, they flip back after a 1-second delay.

**Win/Loss:**
Match all 6 pairs before the 60-second timer hits zero to win ("Shiok!"). If time runs out first, you lose ("Alamak!").

## How it works:

**The Tricks:**
Both players are dealt cards, and a "Trump" suit is chosen. Players must follow the suit led; the highest card wins the trick unless a Trump card is played.

The winner of a trick leads the next card.
